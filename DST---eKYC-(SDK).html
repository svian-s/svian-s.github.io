The DST e-KYC SDK is a specialized solution developed for Brunei Darussalam's DST company. It streamlines onboarding processes by utilizing smart card authentication, facial recognition matching, and OCR technology. This SDK focuses exclusively on enhancing the e-KYC functionality, rather than serving as a comprehensive project.
