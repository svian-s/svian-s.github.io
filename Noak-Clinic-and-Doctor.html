A comprehensive app designed to streamline clinic and doctor management with centralized data, offering an all-in-one solution for healthcare providers. Patients can seamlessly integrate their check-up, investigation, and consultation records across all connected clinics. Key features include inventory management, medical supplies, medication tracking, billing, printing, and medical certificate generation. Built using Jetpack Compose, the app follows best practices with a robust architecture incorporating MVVM, modularization, UDF, and more, ensuring a scalable and efficient solution for healthcare operations.
