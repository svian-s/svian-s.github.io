Prime 360 is an application designed to streamline sales processes for the subsidiary of PT. Nata Connexindo Digital. It accelerates the distribution of lead information from interested customers to the follow-up and transaction stages. The core feature is its CRM (Customer Relationship Management) system, which enables sales teams to efficiently manage customer activities and agreements. Additionally, the app includes a real-time notification feature, alerting sales representatives to new leads or updates, ensuring timely follow-up and improved responsiveness. Additional features :
- Online attendance
- Scheduling (Visit, Meeting, Chat, etc)
- WhatsApp Blast
- Bank Credit Calculator
- Lead Transfer
- Archive Old Lead
