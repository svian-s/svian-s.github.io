Prime 360 is an application used to help sales in subsidiary company of PT. Nata Connexindo Digital named prime 360 to speed up the distribution of lead information from interested customers to the follow-up process and transactions. Its main feature is CRM (Customer Relationship Management) which helps sales manage activities and agreements from customers. This application also has equipped with a notification feature if there is a change in leads / new leads so sales can access on time.
